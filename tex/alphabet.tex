\begin{center}
\LARGE\scshape The Greek Alphabet
\end{center}

The Greek letters were 24, and the required number, 27, was made
up by using \tg{\stigma} (called \bibemph{Stigma}) for 6,
and adding two arbitrary symbols called respectively \bibemph{Koppa},
for 90 and \bibemph{Sampi}, for 900.

The initial forms are shown in square brackets.
